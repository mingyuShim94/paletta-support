Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a color palette?

Open Paletta, go to the Create tab, and tap the color slots to pick colors using the color wheel. You can also extract colors from photos using the camera icon.

How do I extract colors from a photo?

In the Create tab, tap the camera icon to take a photo or select one from your library. Paletta will automatically extract the dominant colors from the image.

Can I copy hex codes?

Yes! Tap any color in your palette to see its hex code, RGB values, and more. Tap the hex code to copy it to your clipboard instantly.

Where are my palettes saved?

All palettes are saved locally on your device in the My Palettes tab. They are stored securely and never uploaded to any server.

Does Paletta require an internet connection?

No. Paletta works completely offline. All color picking and palette creation happens on your device.

Is Paletta free?

Yes. Paletta is completely free with no subscriptions, no watermarks, and no hidden fees.
