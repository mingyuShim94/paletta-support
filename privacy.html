Privacy Policy

Paletta - Color Palette Maker
Last updated: February 23, 2026

Paletta does not collect any personally identifiable information. Your color palettes and photos are processed entirely on your device and never leave it.

Overview

Paletta ("the App") is developed by gguggulab. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data We Do Not Collect

Paletta does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Photos or images you use within the App
  • Color palettes or any user-created content
  • Location data
  • Cookies or advertising identifiers

Analytics Data

Paletta uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics (provided by Google) to help us improve the App. These services may collect:

  • Device identifiers — anonymous identifiers to distinguish unique app installations
  • Product interaction data — which screens are viewed and features used (no personal content)
  • Crash data — technical information about app crashes to help us fix bugs

This data is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally. It is used solely for improving app stability and user experience.

How the App Works

All color palette creation and photo color extraction is performed entirely on your device. The App:

  • Accesses your photo library only when you explicitly choose to extract colors from a photo
  • Accesses your camera only when you choose to capture a photo for color extraction
  • Stores your palettes locally on your device

Photo Library & Camera Access

The App requests access to your photo library and camera solely for the purpose of extracting colors from images. These permissions are optional and requested only when needed. No images are transmitted off your device.

Data Storage

Your color palettes and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is automatically removed when you delete the App.

Third-Party Services

Paletta uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) — anonymous usage analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The anonymous analytics data collected applies equally to all users regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
